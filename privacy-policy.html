1. Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors of rupalimishra.in, the personal portfolio website of Ms. Rupali Mishra. By using this website or initiating contact through its phone, WhatsApp or email links, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

This website does not have signup forms, user accounts, or analytics tracking by default. Personal information is collected only when you voluntarily share it:

  • Phone number — when you call or WhatsApp from the contact links on the website

The website itself does not place tracking cookies or collect personal data automatically. Standard server logs maintained by the website host (such as IP address and basic request metadata) may exist for technical and security purposes, in line with the host’s own privacy practices.

Personal information shared with us is used only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry about a project association mentioned on the website

  • To maintain a record of communications, where reasonably required

4. Who We May Share Information With

Your information may be shared in limited circumstances:

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with marketing lists or third-party advertisers.

5. Data Retention

Personal information shared through enquiries is retained only for as long as is reasonably required to respond to and follow up on the enquiry, or as required under applicable law. You may request deletion of your records at any time using the contact details below.

To exercise any of these rights, email contact@rupalimishra.in with your request and reasonable proof of identity.
